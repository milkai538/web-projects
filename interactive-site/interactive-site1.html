Reality Benders and You: How to Survive

Message to: Reader,

This is a collaborative log between the GOC and SCP organizations meant to explain in layman's terms what a "Type Green" (as denoted by the GOC) or reality bender is, the typical patterns of them, and what to do in the case of meeting each phase.

So, what are the phases of a reality bender?



-- And what else should I know?

As the title claims, this article is about survival. Survival for "Reality Benders and You." Both of our organizations have hired many a reality bender, and still do. We do not plan to change this for any reason. The point in this refusal is coincided with the fact that reality benders are being born at an exponential rate. Again, these are people we are talking about. People who have lives. It is in the primary mission of both of our organizations to keep the veil of normalcy, the survival of humanity, and to better our understanding of the universe. Type Greens follow a science that is as of yet unknown, but we cannot learn it without them, and a wiser humanity is a better humanity.

All credit should be given to the SCP Foundation wikidot, and the community of authors who run it. I did not come up with a single one of these concepts in the slightest.
