Honestly, one of my lesser favored genres. I like it, but not nearly as much as other things I read. Of course, this is only generally speaking. Though, what differs between Osamu Dazai is his unknowing relation to unrealistic fiction. Bungo Stray Dogs is an animanga series that's been ongoing since ~2012, and features the names and books of numerous real, dead authors, excluding one named after a living and modern author. Osamu Dazai, the pen name which he is referred to by, is one of these characters, along with his book's name, "No Longer Human".
