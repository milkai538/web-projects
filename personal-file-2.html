The Premise

Alright, let's do this one last time; The SCP Foundation is a fictional organization which contains & studies anomalous beings, artifacts, events, etc. Although sometimes a baseline "canon" may be referred to, there is none. Different stories have different canons that all are made to fit the story, and this extends to any GoI(Group of Interest) mentioned. Not to be mistaken with "Do whatever just use the title" as there are standards, and consistenly recurring themes. So to break it down: Each SCP story has a required head to use, and the rest of the entire body is as the writer pleases. Do you want the Foundation to be hundreds of years old? Done. Just a decade or so? Done. Is the Foundation extremely very strict about how it works, their motto, and their actions? Only if you need it to be that strict, but don't get it too different the site has rules too and the Foundation has standards. I could go on about what's flexible, or you can do that yourself. (General) Ranks of Order

  • The Administrator - Top of the top of the top. Literally. Created the place, and context of him/her/it/ can be changed, except shrouded in mystery.
  • The O5 (Overseer) council - Usually the top of the top. Very mysterious and thus flexible. Usually 13 members, or well 13 numbers. Apparently in some canons multiple people share the number. They may be human, or just be humanoids. They do have anomalous effects as well, that's consistent, but a story can remove that. Sometimes the numbers are a rank of personal order inside the council. You get it, very flexible and mysterious. The 5 in their group name comes from their security level which I'll also get into. Only they may have Level 5 keycards.
